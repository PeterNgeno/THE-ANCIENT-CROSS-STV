Who We Are

THE ANCIENT CROSS STV is a Christian media platform dedicated to proclaiming the message of Jesus Christ through music and creative visual expression.

We are not a church or a religious institution. We are a digital platform committed to producing content that strengthens faith, encourages repentance, inspires holiness, and reminds believers of the eternal hope found in Christ.

Every song, video, and message is created with one central purpose: to lift up the Cross and point hearts back to God.

Our Mission

Our mission is to use media as a tool for spiritual awakening in a generation surrounded by noise and distraction.

Through worship music, reflective messages, and Christ-centered storytelling, we seek to:

• Call believers to deeper intimacy with God
• Encourage readiness for the return of Christ
• Promote biblical truth without compromise
• Inspire faithfulness in challenging times

What We Create

THE ANCIENT CROSS STV produces:

• Original worship songs and gospel compositions
• Lyric videos and visual worship experiences
• Prophetic and reflective Christian content
• Digital media centered on Scripture and spiritual growth

This is media with eternal purpose — not entertainment without meaning.

About the Creator

THE ANCIENT CROSS STV was founded and is managed by CREATOR KING (Peter Simon) — a gospel songwriter, worship musician, and digital creator.

With a passion for biblical truth and spiritual preparation, the vision behind this platform is to awaken hearts through worship and call believers to steadfast faith as the world moves toward its prophetic fulfillment.
