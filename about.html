Who We Are

THE ANCIENT CROSS STV is a Christian media platform, not a church or a ministry. We focus on producing and sharing prophetic worship music, gospel videos, and visual messages that point people back to Christ.

Our content is created to encourage reflection, repentance, hope, and readiness for the coming of the Messiah through music and inspired storytelling.

What We Do

• Produce original worship songs and gospel music videos
• Share prophetic and reflective Christian messages
• Use media, sound, and visuals to communicate biblical truth
• Publish content on YouTube and digital platforms

This is media with a message — not entertainment without purpose.

About the Creator

THE ANCIENT CROSS STV is created and managed by CREATOR KING (Peter Kipsangngeno), a gospel songwriter, worship musician, and digital creator.

Through music and media, the channel calls believers to watchfulness and faithfulness as the world approaches the final days.
